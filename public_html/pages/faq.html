FAQs

Call us for any information. Mail us for any query. Visit us to chechout our wonderous premises and to get enrolled.

What is the admission procedure?

For those seeking admissions, an informal interaction will be conducted in whichpupil and both the parents have to be present. The final decision of the admission committee will be binding on PACE.

What is the fee structure?

Please contact the institute for details

What curriculum will the institute follow?

P. N. Institute follows GSEB/CBSE curriculum

How many terms will the institute have?

We will be having two/three terms as per GSEB/CBSE

What will be the institute timings?

Institute timings are: 7:45 am to 3:00 pm

Will the institute authorities be taking adequate measures to ensure hygiene in the institute?

P. N. Institute maintains a high standard of cleanliness and hygiene. There are regular checks and monitoring by the institute administration as well as the coordinator of the institute.

What is the kind of security offered to the students?

The institute has installed CCTV in all classrooms. Students are always accompanied by teachers or the authority when they are moving from one class to the other.

How many students will be there per division?

We strive to maintain a ratio of 30 Students in each class

Do you have career counselling in your institute?

Yes, Institute is accompanied by Career Counsellors & Career Counselling Cell (CCC) ensure that students are placed in the best Universities in India and abroad.

Are you participating in Inter school, National and International level competitions?

Yes, to view the results of all the different competitions please visit RESULT section of our website.

How do you take care of students who are weak in a particular subject?

Students are asked to stay back at times for remedial classes to provide them extra support, whenever required. A regular feedback regarding the homework and progress of students, keeps parents well-informed and ensures a closely monitored development programme for their children.
